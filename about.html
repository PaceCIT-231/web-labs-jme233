Lady Pink was born in Ecuador, raised in NYC and currently resides in the
north of the city. In 1979 she started writing graffiti and soon was well known
as the only female capable of competing with the boys in the graffiti subculture.
Pink painted subway trains from the years 1979-1985. In 1982 she had a starring role
the motion picture "Wild Style". That role and her other significant contributions to
graffiti have made her a cult figure in the hip-hop subculture.
